Kindle Highlight Exporter

Export your Kindle highlights without the usual mess.

Drag one bookmarklet into your bookmarks bar, open Kindle Notebook, and download a local ZIP of markdown files organized by author. No backend. No account. No syncing service. Just your highlights and notes in plain text.

What you get One markdown file per book, with notes and highlights preserved and ZIP paths under `kindle-highlights/<author>/...`.
How it runs The bookmarklet runs in your browser session against Kindle Notebook and builds the ZIP locally before downloading it.

Step 1

Drag this into your bookmarks bar.

Install the bookmarklet once, then use it from Kindle Notebook any time you want a fresh export.

Export Kindle Highlights

Keep the button in your bookmarks bar so you can launch the export directly from Kindle Notebook.

Steps 2-4

Run the export.

  1. Open Kindle Notebook Visit read.amazon.com/notebook while logged in to Amazon.
  2. Click the bookmarklet Use Export Kindle Highlights from your bookmarks bar to start the scrape and export flow.
  3. Download your zip A ZIP file downloads automatically with one markdown file per book, grouped by author.

Sample Output

Markdown that is actually usable.
